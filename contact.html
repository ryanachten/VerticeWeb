Vertice was developed by Ryan Achten and Tim Bathgate between June and November, 2016.

Vertice was fortunate to receive support and funding from Victoria University of Wellington’s commercialisation office, VicLink, alongside the Kiwi Innovation Network (KiwiNet). Additionally, the advice and mentorship provided by Michael Elwood Smith from Loomio.
